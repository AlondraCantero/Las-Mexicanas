About Our and Your Store

El Noa Noa was opened by Elodia Cantero and Nancy Lopez in 2014. As Hispanics living in the United States we know how it feels to miss out on our Hispanic culture and foods, because it is amazing.

Therefore, we decided to bring our Hispanic culture and roots here to Jackson, Tennessee, so you and anyone can enjoy it with us. El Noa Noa is not only a Mexican store but a Latin American store. Living in the United States every Hispanic is categorized as Mexicans, but as Hispanics we ALL stand together. When you walk into El Noa Noa you will see all of the different Latin American flags including the United States flag, because the United States are our home and we are Americans.
